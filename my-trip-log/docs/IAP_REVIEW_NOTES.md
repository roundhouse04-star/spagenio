## ✅ App Store Connect "심사용 추가 정보(Review Notes)" 칸에 붙여넣기 (영문 권장)

```
This is a non-consumable in-app purchase that permanently removes banner
advertisements from Triplive, a personal travel-journal app. It is a one-time
purchase (NOT a subscription) and unlocks lifetime ad-free use.

How to locate and test the purchase:
1. Launch the app and complete the short onboarding (nickname + agreements).
2. A banner ad area is shown above the tab bar on the main screens.
3. Tap the "Remove Ads (광고 제거)" button on the banner, OR
   go to the "My Info (마이)" tab -> "Triplive PRO" menu.
4. The PRO screen shows the price and a "Purchase" button.
5. After a successful purchase, all banner ads are removed across the entire app.
6. "Restore Purchases" is available on the same screen for users who reinstall
   or switch devices.

Notes:
- One-time payment, lifetime access. No subscription, no recurring billing.
- Purchases are validated server-side via RevenueCat.
- No account/login required; the purchase is tied to the user's Apple ID.
```
